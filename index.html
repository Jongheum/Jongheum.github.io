Biography

I graduated from Seoul National University with B.S. in Physics. I am a researcher working at Quantum Gas Lab, advised by Prof. Yong-il Shin. My research passion centers on leveraging ultracold platforms for diverse applications: quantum computation, quantum simula- tion, exotic phases in quantum many-body physics, precise measurement, and other novel phenomena to expand the horizon of physics.

Interests

  • Ultracold quantum gases
  • Quantum simulation and computation
  • Complex quantum system
  • Condensed matter physics
  • Strongly correlated/entangled systems
  • Spin mixtures
  • Critical vortex shedding
  • Precise measurement
